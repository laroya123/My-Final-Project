Company Profile

The demand for globalization requires the development of skilled and fully educated human resources to meet the ever changing challenges for aachieving competitive labor force. To attain these, all learning institution must endeavor to harness the potentioals of their students to attain competitive graduates. Toward this end, a higher educational institution (HEI) was established after a survey was conducted by way of random sampling of parents from La Union and from the north and western towns of Pangasinan whose children are in elementary and secondary schools and could be prospective students of the proposed HEI.

After the organizational meeting of the incorporators, the organizartion came up with the name Philippine College of Northwestern Luzon (PCNL) with the articles of incorporation aand by - laws duly approved by the Securities and Exchange Commision, Baguio Extension office on the 20 day of February 2007.

All Ched requirements were prepaired by the group including the province prospective courses to be offered namely Bachelor of Science in Business Administration (BSBA), Bachelor of Science in Accountancy (BSA), Bachelor of Science in Education (BSE), Bachelor in Elementary Education (BEE), and Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT). While the institution was waiting for the approval of Commision on Higher Education(CHED), the Board of Trustees composed of 9 members of the 15 incorporators likewise preferred the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA) requirements for offering short term courses. After the visit of TESDA technical panel, the institution was granted a permit to offer four short term courses namely Computer Hardware Servicing NC II, PC Operations NC II, and Tourism Housekeeping NS II which started the 1st semester of school year 2007-2008.

On February 2008, the courses approved by CHED are BSBA and BSA to be offered first during the first semester of school year 2008-2009. The following yearm BSE and BEE were approved followed by BSIT and CRIMINOLOGY respectively.

The Teacher Education Programs are designed to practice educators who will develop a literature and enumarate mass base needed in basic education program. BSBA and its major areas, financial management and HRDM, are percieved as contributors to the business and entrpreneurial ventures whichh are the building blocks to an industrialized economy becouse of their broad global outlook to understand the context in which business enterprises operate. The BSIT and its major areas like computer programming that aims to contribute in innovating new technologies to make more efficient and easier living world. The Criminology aims to contributeand maintain the peace and order to our growing community and countri. BSA together with its major areas that provides a good and better banking world. The TESDA courses provide very high potential to persue a college degree.

PCML located in a environment conductive to learning and developing. It is away from places of questionable character such as gambling establishments, drinking joints and videooke bars. There are no manufacturing and industrial establishment near the school and science laboratories. There are 11 classrooms, a housekeeping room and spacious library and in front is an open court. Based on its 5-year development plan(2007-2012) the college will continue constructing and upgrading its facilities. The initial investment was P3,900,900.00.

PCNL expected to be social desirable and economically viable for the reason that academic courses shall increase the number of frofessionals and technical people in the region and will contribute to human resources pooling for local and foreign employment.
