Admission Requirement

Sec. 1.Freshman Student

a.A student shall be admitted regardless of age, sex, nationality, religious beliefs, socio-economic status or political afflications

b.A student must have a graduated from a government recognized secondary school of alternative learning system.

c.The student must meet all the following admission requirements:

i.Form 138 or Alternative Learning System(ALS) Rating.

ii.Certificate of Good Moral Character

iii.Copy of Birth Certificate form the National Statistic Office.

iv.Three (3) copiea 2*2 and 1 copy 1*1 colored pictures with white background.

Sec.2.Foreign Student

a. A foreign student must meet all the following admission requirements of the colloge and the course applied for:

i.Certificate of Complition of Secondary Curriculum

ii.Original Transcript of Records

iii.Personal Date

iv.Affidavit of Support

v.Alien Certificate of Registration(ACR)

vi.Result of TOELF/IELTS(for non-native speakers of English)

vii.Studen Visa

viii.Security Clearance from his/her embassy

ix.Resident Guarantor of his/her character

x.CHED Permit

xi.Alien Fee

b. A foreign student must be admitted based on availability of slot in the course applied for.

Sec.3.Tranferees

a. A transferees must meet the following admission requirement of the college

i. Official Transcript of Record(OTR) or copy of grades signed by the Registrar for reference purposes

ii. Tranfer Credentials

iii. Certificate of Good Moral Character

iv. Four(4) colored ID pictures with white background (1 copy 1*1 and # copies 2*2

b.Admission Tranferees is subject to availability of slot for quota courses

General Objectives

2.

To offer relevant profesional and Technical-Vocational Education and Training (TVET) courses/programs that will enhance the social and economic development of the region and the country;

3.

To discover and develop the diverse talents and potentials of its clients thereby contributing to theie holistic development;

4.

To work for the advancement of knowledge and the technology through research;

5

To participate in goverment efforts to improve the quality of life through extension
